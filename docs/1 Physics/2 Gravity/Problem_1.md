## Derivation of Kepler's Third Law

### Step 1: Gravitational and Centripetal Forces

Newton's Law of Gravitation:

$$
F_g = \frac{G M m}{r^2}
$$

Centripetal force for circular motion:  

$$
F_c = m \frac{v^2}{r}
$$

Equating $$F_g = F_c$$ :

$$
\frac{G M m}{r^2} = m \frac{v^2}{r}
$$

Canceling $$m$$ :

$$
\frac{G M}{r^2} = \frac{v^2}{r}
$$

Multiplying by $$r$$:

$$
v^2 = \frac{G M}{r}
$$
